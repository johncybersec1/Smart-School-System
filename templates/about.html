About Us

Who We Are

SmartSchool is a student-driven initiative designed to revolutionize how educational institutions manage their daily operations.

What We Do

We simplify workflows, enhance communication, and create intuitive solutions for students, educators, and administrators.

Our Vision

To build smarter, tech-driven educational environments that empower institutions worldwide to achieve excellence.

Our Journey

2021

SmartSchool was conceptualized by two students at WSPA University, driven by the need for smarter educational management solutions.

2023

Prototype development and initial testing began across selected educational institutions in Poland.

2024

SmartSchool is ready for global implementation, with partnerships established across multiple institutions.
